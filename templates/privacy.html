Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our security services. This includes:

  • Account Information: Your email address and password (encrypted) for authentication.
  • Scan Data: Target URLs, scan history, and vulnerability findings generated during your use of the platform.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses and browser information for security monitoring and abuse prevention.

2. Usage of Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate security reports and provide risk intelligence.
  • Communicate critical security alerts or account updates.
  • Improve our detection engine using anonymized, aggregated scan results.

3. Security Practices

Vulnix employs industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We conduct regular internal security audits of our infrastructure to ensure your scan data remains confidential.

4. No Selling of Data

We have a strict policy: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data or scan findings to third parties. Your security posture is your business alone.

5. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at: vulnix.space@gmail.com
